About Me

Since my first international flight at the age of 10 months old, I have travelled to various destinations with my family, school and friends.

I have always enjoyed these trips, and love to document them through the photographs and videos that I take. It is always a special experience to look back through my camera roll, and photos allow you to relive a memory in a way that you may not otherwise be able to achieve.

Most recently I have travelled around New Zealand, Japan, and Australia, and so on this website I have curated the best of my photos from these three beautiful countries.

New Zealand

Since returning from Japan in 2009, I have lived in New Zealand with my family. Below are a selection of photographs from around the country.

Japan

I lived in Japan with my family until the age of 8, and my father continues to spend half of each year there although we have moved back to New Zealand. As a result of this I have a very personal connection to Japan, and our family continues to vist the country every few years.

Australia

Despite it's proximity to New Zealand, I had never travelled to Australia before September last year (2018). After visiting Sydney in September, we returned as a family in December and spent Christmas in Melbourne.
